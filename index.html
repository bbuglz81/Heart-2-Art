Hello! I'm Sarah Engelskirchen-Bugler.

I'm a Front-End Developer focused on building responsive web pages that users want to explore.

I've worked in a number of positions in the past, but what connects them is that I have often played a role in evolution and development. When I’m away from the computer, I’m probably exploring the PNW, cheering on the Seattle Storm, or just relaxing with my wife and our fur babies.
